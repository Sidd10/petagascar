services

Bark In Style (Pet Salon): Take your loved one from stinky to stunning !! Located at andheri (w), we offer pick ups & drops to make it as convenient as possible for you. The grooming area is clean, well ventilated and even equipped with streaming music to give your pet the best experience possible.

Knock Knock (In-home Grooming): Your precious pet will appreciate being groomed in the comfort of his/her familiar home environment. We bathe your pet in your bathroom and set up our table just about anywhere in reach of an electrical outlet. We bring along everything from our mobile grooming table to the specialized equipment required. Rest assured you will not have to clean up after us !
